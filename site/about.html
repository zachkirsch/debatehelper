About

DebateHelper is a Microsoft Word based template based on the Verbatim template, but redesigned for Public Forum debate. It includes an elaborate macro system for collecting evidence, keeping it organized, and accessing it during a debate round.

The purpose of DebateHelper is to allow Public Forum debaters to not be overwhelmed by excessive amounts of evidence. By staying organized and keeping track of a large volumes of information, any team regardless of size can be successful.
